We’re Growing: Aesthetic Tile Is Coming to Groveland

Published on August 15, 2025 | By the Aesthetic Tile Team

Big news: we’re opening our second location in Groveland, Florida—and I’m genuinely thrilled. This move has been in my heart for a while. Originally from Louisville, we vacationed here last year and ever since then I’ve thought: this is where we’re meant to grow. Groveland has the kind of energy that makes you want to do great work—friendly faces, hard-working families, and a pride in homes and small businesses that reminds me why I started in the first place.

Why We Chose Groveland

Choosing to plant roots here wasn’t a numbers decision—it was about people. This community reminded us of home, and it was clear that a local tile company focused on relationships and reliability could make a real impact. From the first visits, we felt welcomed and encouraged by homeowners who take pride in their spaces.

A second location isn’t just about space or numbers. It’s about being around the kind of people that I want my kids to grow up around. It’s about being around businesses with the same values that we have: honest estimates, thoughtful guidance, and craftsmanship that holds up to Florida weather and daily life. If you’ve worked with us before, you know we treat every project like it’s our own.

Our Commitment to the Community

We’re arriving in Lake County ready to listen, serve, and support the neighbors who welcomed us so quickly. As a trusted tile contractor Groveland residents can rely on, our mission is to deliver craftsmanship that elevates every home or business we touch while building long-term relationships.

Here’s to the start of a new chapter—one built on amazing relationships. Thank you to everyone who encouraged us and kept asking, “When are you coming to Groveland?” The answer is: now.

How Our Groveland Team Can Help

When you need a tile installation Groveland experts trust, we’re here for backsplashes, bathrooms, flooring, fireplaces, and more. Our crew pairs design insight with meticulous execution so your finished project looks incredible and performs for years.
