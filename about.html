About Aesthetic Tile — Groveland, Florida

                   

Family owned. Florida based. Three generations strong.

                   

At Aesthetic Tile, tile isn't just what we do—it's who we are. Our craft began with our grandfather, was refined by our father, and today is led by two brothers carrying that legacy forward across Central Florida. With more than a decade of professional tile installation experience and a multi generation heritage of hands on craftsmanship, we combine old school precision with modern design to deliver spaces that stand the test of time.

               
               
                   
                       
                           

Our Story

                           

What started as a family trade has grown into a Florida based tile company trusted by homeowners, designers, and builders. Over three generations, we've honed the details that matter: straight lines, tight grout joints, true surfaces, and meticulous prep. That standard is why clients consistently describe our work as "perfectionist," "detail oriented," and "the very best."

                       
                       
                                                   
                   
               
               
                   

What We Do

                   

We deliver custom tile installation with clean execution and lasting results. Core services include:

                   
                           
  • Bathroom tile installation: from classic layouts to statement features and spa like showers.
    •                        
  • Kitchen backsplashes: durable, easy to clean surfaces that elevate everyday cooking spaces.
    •                        
  • Tile repairs & rework: crack fixes, grout issues, and water damage remediation to restore beauty and function.
    •                        
  • Demolition & surface prep: tidy removal and prep that protect your home and set the stage for a flawless install.
    •                    
                   

From intricate mosaics to complex, large projects, we approach every job with the same uncompromising attention to detail—because great tile work is never an accident. It's the result of discipline, patience, and craft.

               
           
       
               
           
               

Frequently Asked Questions

               
                   

Do you offer free estimates? How do I get started?

                   

Yes. Submit the Request a Quote form on our site or reach us at office@aesthetictile.com or (502) 650-7014. We're available Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm.

               
               
                   

What services do you specialize in?

                   

We focus on custom tile installation (bathrooms, showers, backsplashes), tile repairs (cracked tiles, grout issues, water damage fixes), and demolition/surface prep with a clean, precise process—so the space is ready for the next phase.

               
               
                   

Where are you located, and which areas do you serve?

                   

We're based in Groveland, Florida at 1195 Greenley Ave, Groveland, FL 34736, serving homeowners and builders across Central Florida. Contact us to confirm service to your city.

               
               
                   

How experienced is your team?

                   

We're a third generation tile family (grandfather → father → now two brothers) with over a decade of hands on installation experience and a legacy "passed down through generations."

               
               
                   

How do you handle demolition, dust control, and cleanup?

                   

Demolition is part of our service. We perform clean, precise demo and maintain a tidy jobsite so your home stays protected and ready for installation.
