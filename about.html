About Aesthetic Tile: Expert Tile Contractors in Central Florida

Family owned. Florida based. Three generations strong.

Our Story: A Legacy of Craftsmanship Moves to Groveland

The foundation of Aesthetic Tile rests on three generations of specialized knowledge. What began as a dedicated family trade has evolved into a professional tile company trusted by discerning homeowners, interior designers, and custom builders who demand superior quality.

A New Chapter in the Sunshine State

For years, we honed our craft in Kentucky, focusing on high-end residential projects and complex installations. However, we saw an opportunity to bring our commitment to excellence to the vibrant communities of Florida. We are thrilled to have established our new headquarters right here in Groveland, FL.

As proud new residents of Lake County, we are excited to offer our expertise and services to Central Florida. This move allows us to combine our traditional craftsmanship with the unique architectural styles and design trends of the Orlando area.

The Aesthetic Tile Difference

  • Meticulous Preparation: A lasting installation starts beneath the surface. We ensure every substrate is sound, waterproof, and perfectly prepared.
  • Technical Precision: We specialize in achieving laser-straight lines, tight and consistent grout joints, and truly flat surfaces.
  • Lasting Results: We don’t cut corners, ensuring your investment remains durable and impressive for years to come.

We look forward to serving our new neighbors in Groveland, Clermont, Winter Garden, and beyond, continuing our legacy of excellence, one tile at a time.

What We Do

We deliver custom tile installation with clean execution and lasting results. Core services include:

  • Bathroom tile installation: from classic layouts to statement features and spa like showers.
  • Kitchen backsplashes: durable, easy to clean surfaces that elevate everyday cooking spaces.
  • Tile repairs & rework: crack fixes, grout issues, and water damage remediation to restore beauty and function.
  • Demolition & surface prep: tidy removal and prep that protect your home and set the stage for a flawless install.

From intricate mosaics to complex, large projects, we approach every job with the same uncompromising attention to detail—because great tile work is never an accident. It's the result of discipline, patience, and craft.
