A New Chapter

Big news: we're opening our second location in Groveland, Florida—and I'm genuinely thrilled.

This move has been in my heart for a while. Originally from Louisville, we vacationed here last year and ever since then I've thought: this is where we're meant to grow. Groveland has the kind of energy that makes you want to do great work—friendly faces, hard-working families, and a pride in homes and small businesses that reminds me why I started in the first place.

A second location isn't just about space or numbers. It's about being around the kind of people that I want my kids to grow up around. It's about being around businesses with the same values that we have: honest estimates, thoughtful guidance, and craftsmanship that holds up to Florida weather and daily life. If you've worked with us before, you know we treat every project like it's our own.

Here's to the start of a new chapter—one built on amazing relationships. Thank you to everyone who encouraged us and kept asking, "When are you coming to Groveland?" The answer is: now.

If you're planning a refresh, tackling a repair, or just exploring ideas, stop by and say hello. We're excited to meet more of our new Groveland neighbors, take on meaningful projects, and prove—one job at a time—that local, dependable service still matters. Here's to growing roots and doing great work right here at home.
